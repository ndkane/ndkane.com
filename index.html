Hello! My name is Natalie and I’m a Web Designer. I’m still learning, but I suspect everyone always is. I’m also an Artist, and have been known to run workshops and projects that aim to make Art inclusive, democratic and fun. I also like to write about Art and Digital culture too.

I want (to work with) you! I’m interested in developing my craft with creative people who share my love for Responsive and Semantic Web Design. I’m very friendly (promise), and have a commitment to open, honest conversations about design. If you’d like to work with me, get in touch!

Content Out Web Development — I learned how to do this Web thing through my lessons with Aral Balkan. We kept track of our lessons so that you can learn too. I’ve kept the design the same as it was when I was learning, so that there’s a better reference for what I’m talking about!
